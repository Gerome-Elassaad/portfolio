## Terms Related to DeepRankAI

To fully grasp the capabilities and benefits of DeepRankAI, it's useful to familiarize yourself with some commonly used terms in AI detection and content humanization:

- **AI Content Detection:** The process of identifying text that has been generated by artificial intelligence systems.
- **Content Humanization:** Techniques used to make AI-generated text appear more natural and human-like.
- **Linguistic Markers:** Specific patterns or characteristics in text that indicate AI generation.
- **Natural Language Processing (NLP):** Technology that helps computers understand and process human language.
- **Writing Authenticity:** The degree to which content appears to be genuinely written by a human author.
- **Detection Algorithms:** Automated systems designed to identify AI-generated content.
- **Sentence Fluency:** The natural flow and rhythm of written sentences that mimics human speech.
- **Stylistic Analysis:** Examination of writing style elements like tone, vocabulary, and structure.
- **Perplexity Scoring:** A measurement used to assess how predictable or unpredictable text appears.
- **Human-like Patterns:** Writing characteristics that are typical of natural human communication.
